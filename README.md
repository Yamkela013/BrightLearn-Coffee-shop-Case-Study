Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis
Data Analysis | SQL | Databricks | Excel Dashboards
📌 Overview
An end‑to‑end sales analytics project analyzing transactional data from Bright Coffee Shop to identify revenue drivers, product performance, and time‑based sales trends.
The project focuses on turning raw sales data into actionable insights to support executive decision‑making and revenue growth.

🎯 Objectives

Identify top‑revenue and best‑selling products
Analyse peak vs slow sales periods
Understand sales trends across time and store locations
Deliver data‑driven recommendations for revenue improvement


🛠️ Tools & Skills

Databricks – Data processing & SQL analysis
SQL – Transformations, aggregations & KPIs
Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets – Pivot tables & dashboards
PowerPoint / Canva – Executive reporting
Miro – Data flow & architecture planning

🔍 Key Insights

Revenue shows steady month‑on‑month growth across all locations
Coffee and Tea are the primary revenue drivers
Morning time periods generate the highest sales
Sales performance is consistent and predictable over time


✅ Recommendations

Prioritise inventory and staffing during morning peak hours
Promote best‑selling beverage products
Introduce time‑based offers during slow periods
Review underperforming products for optimisation or removal
